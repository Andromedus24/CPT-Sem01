Black Jack

Select The Number of Players!


The goal of the game is to get 21
or as close as you can to it under 21.
The card values are shown below. If
you get over 21, you automatically bust,
which means you cannot win. If there is a
tie, then all players under the tie win.

Note that the ace is NOT STATIC. It will change values from an
11 or a 1 depending on your current hand
